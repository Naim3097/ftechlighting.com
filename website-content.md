## PAGE 01: CORPORATE INFORMATION

### Hero Section
**Company Name:** FTECH Solutions Sdn Bhd

### About FTECH
FTECH Solutions Sdn Bhd (FTECH) is a holistic and innovative lighting specialist headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The company provides end-to-end lighting solutions, working closely with clients from conceptualization and design planning to lighting simulation, project management, installation, and maintenance.

FTECH is a licensed professional entity specializing in bespoke luminaires, pole customization, and steel and aluminium fabrication works for construction projects within the commercial and industrial sectors.

The company's capabilities also encompass the design and installation of both indoor and outdoor lighting systems for various facilities, including office buildings, public transportation terminals, convention and event centers, educational institutions, libraries, and parliamentary complexes.

### Mission Statement
At FTECH, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality, reliable, and sustainable lighting solutions that meet the functional and aesthetic needs of our clients while upholding professional standards and excellence in every project undertaken.
